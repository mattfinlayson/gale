<p></p><p><a href="http://stevenf.tumblr.com/post/391510844/a-homebrew-app-for-the-palm-pre-reboots-your-phone">stevenf</a>:</p><p>A homebrew app for the Palm Pre <a href="http://www.precentral.net/reboot-schedule-homebrew-app-does-reboots-your-schedule">reboots your phone according to a schedule</a>.</p><p>This is such a perfectly encapsulated nutshell of exactly why Apple does not allow third-party background processes on the iPhone.</p><p>On the other hand, a Pre can play music from Pandora in the background while you do something else.</p><p>This gets right to the very heart of the old world / new world schism.</p>
