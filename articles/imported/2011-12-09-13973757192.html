Baroque.me: J.S. Bach - Cello Suite No. 1 - Prelude by Alexander Chen // Baroque.me (2011) by Alexander Chen. Video capture. http://bit.ly/uy6C1C visualizes the first Prelude from Bach’s Cello Suites. Using the math behind string length and pitch, it came from a simple idea: what if all the notes were drawn as strings? Instead of a stream of classical notation on a page, this interactive project highlights the music’s underlying structure and subtle shifts.
