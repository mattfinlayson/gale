PSA of the Day: The new version of the now-legendary Super Mario Crossover has finally dropped. Aside from ushering in the era of Ryu Hayabusa, here’s what’s new in v1.1:

Mega Man now has Rush Coil, Samus Aran now has crouching ability, Simon Belmont gets better jumping ability, Link can now attack in more directions, Mario now gets a weird version where everything plays likes it’s underwater, the game itself has difficulty settings, [and] cheats have been added.

Check out the version history page for a complete list of changes.
