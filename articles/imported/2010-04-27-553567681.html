Longform story: condensed. 

One of the joys of e-readers (ala Kindle and iPad) is the format allows for different lengths of publications. In other words it’s hard to sell a 15 - 30 page book, it’s hard to print a magazine with a 30 page story, but the new medium provides a landing place for this kind of expression. 

Longform.org pulls this type of story from instapaper and makes a landing page of likely material that you can push back into instapaper and consume through the web, or something like your kindle. 

Lovely.
