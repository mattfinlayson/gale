On the day that [Edison] first lit up a park with his new electric light, a reporter came up to him and asked him, ‘Do you think that someday ordinary people will be able to afford electric lights?’ He is reported to have replied, ‘One day, only the rich will be able to afford candles.’
—Comment on Books in the Age of the iPad. (via inky)
